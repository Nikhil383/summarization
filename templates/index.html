About This Tool

This text summarization tool uses advanced AI models to generate concise summaries of your text. Different models are optimized for different types of content:

  • BART-large-CNN: Best for news articles and general content
  • PEGASUS-XSUM: Excellent for extreme summarization with high compression
  • BART-large-XSUM: Optimized for single-sentence summaries

The tool automatically cleans your text and provides statistics about the summarization process.
